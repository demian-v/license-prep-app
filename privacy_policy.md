## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
We collect the following personal information when you use our App:
- **Account Information:** Name, email address, and password when you create an account
- **Profile Preferences:** Selected state for DMV content, language preference
- **Subscription Data:** Subscription status, billing information, purchase history

### 2.2 Usage Data
We automatically collect information about how you use our App:
- **Learning Progress:** Quiz scores, practice test results, study progress, saved questions
- **App Analytics:** Screen views, feature usage, session duration, app performance metrics
- **Device Information:** App version, platform type (iOS/Android), device model
- **Session Data:** Login times, session IDs for security purposes

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
We do NOT collect:
- Location data or GPS information
- Camera or photo access
- Microphone or audio recordings
- Contact lists or phone numbers
- SMS or call log data
- Health or biometric information

## 5. Data Security and Storage

### 5.1 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:
- **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
- **Secure Authentication:** Firebase Authentication provides secure login systems
- **Access Controls:** Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
- **Regular Security Reviews:** Ongoing monitoring and security assessments

### 5.2 Data Storage
- Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase cloud infrastructure
- Data is stored in secure data centers with appropriate physical and technical safeguards
- We implement backup and disaster recovery procedures

### 5.3 Data Retention
- **Account Data:** Retained as long as your account is active
- **Usage Analytics:** Retained for up to 2 years for app improvement purposes
- **Subscription Data:** Retained as required for billing and tax purposes
- **Deleted Accounts:** All personal data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Account Management
- **Access:** You can view and update your account information within the App
- **Correction:** You can correct inaccurate personal information in your profile
- **Account Deletion:** You can delete your account and all associated data through the App settings

### 6.2 Data Deletion Process
To delete your account and all associated data:
1. Open the DriveUSA app
2. Go to Profile/Settings
3. Select "Delete Account"
4. Confirm your request
5. All your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days

### 6.3 Communication Preferences
- You can opt out of promotional emails by following unsubscribe links
- Essential service communications (billing, security) cannot be opted out of while maintaining an active account

### 6.4 Analytics Opt-Out
- iOS users can limit ad tracking through device settings
- Android users can reset or opt out of analytics tracking through Google settings

### 7.1 Right to Know

### 7.2 Right to Delete
You can request deletion of personal information we have collected about you (subject to certain exceptions).

### 7.3 Right to Opt-Out
You have the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information. **Note: We do not sell personal information.**

### 7.4 Non-Discrimination
We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us at driveusaservice@driveusallc.com.

## 8. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

DriveUSA is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information immediately.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at driveusaservice@driveusallc.com.
